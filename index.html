Classic Cappuccino

Ingredients

  • Shot of espresso
  • Cup of whole milk
  • Cup of heavy cream
  • Tablespoon of granulated sugar
  • Cocoa powder or cinnamon for garnish

Recipe

Indulge in a classic cappuccino by brewing a strong espresso shot, frothing whole milk, topping with sweet whipped cream, and garnishing with cocoa or cinnamon.

Classic Butterscotch Cake

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter or oil
  • Baking powder or baking soda

Recipe

Whip up a delicious cake with simple ingredients: Combine all-purpose flour, sugar, eggs, and butter. Add baking powder for a light texture. Mix, pour into a greased pan, and bake until golden. Frost or enjoy plain. A delightful homemade treat ready to satisfy your sweet cravings!

Classic Grilled Burger

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Bun
  • Tomato
  • Cheese

Recipe

For a delicious homemade burger, mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Form patties and cook on a hot grill for 4-5 minutes per side. Toast buns, spread mayo, and add lettuce, tomato, and cheese. Top with ketchup and mustard. Enjoy this mouthwatering burger in just a few simple steps!
